    Grady Financial Assistance Program

    Grady offers discounted care under a Financial Assistance Program to qualified individuals for emergency and medically necessary services. Grady does not discriminate in the provision of emergency or medically necessary care based on ability to pay or source of payment. If you need information about financial assistance or an application form visit the billing and insurance page: https://www.gradyhealth.org/billing-insurance/
